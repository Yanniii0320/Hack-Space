Ni Yan

Hi, this is Nyan, an artist who strives to push the boundaries of visual communication through the use of computer technology. My aim is to discover new ways to enhance the exchange of information between people by exploring the fluidity of both information and graphics. I enjoy the process of merging these elements and capturing them in the form of interactive physical installations.
